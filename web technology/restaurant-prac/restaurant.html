About us

Taking inspiration from Shizusan's travels, the restaurant offers dishes that reflect the South East Asian ingredients and flavours in their truest forms. Breaking away from the convention, one can find dishes that resemble art and design, along with a comfortable and aesthetic touch at Shizusan. He's set to offer the best dishes with ingredients of the highest quality combined with restraint and balance. Here one can even indulge in an array of handcrafted cocktails that are true to their origin, being quite different from what one might find at their around-the-corner Pan- Asian restaurant.
